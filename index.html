I'm using category theory to understand supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and game theory.

I'm a third-year PhD student in the Mathematically Structured Programming Group at the University of Strathclyde, Glasgow, United Kingdom. My supervisor is Neil Ghani.

My long-term goal is understanding the fundamental principles of intelligence. For a detailed description of my research interests and the philosophy behind them, check out the Research Philosophy page. Most of my programming work can be found on Github. I am also active on Twitter.
